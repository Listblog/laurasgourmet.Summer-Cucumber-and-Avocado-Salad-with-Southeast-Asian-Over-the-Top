Summer Cucumber and Avocado Salad with Southeast Asian Over the Top Recipe
By Chef Laura Briscoe
Prep time: 10 minutes Cook time: 0 minutes Servings: 2-3 servings

Ingredients

- 1/2 of one large hot house (English) cucumber

- 1 large avocado, peeled, pitted and thinly sliced

- 1 medium serrano, thinly sliced (seeded, if less spice is desired)

- 1 large scallion, thinly sliced

- 1 heaping tablespoon, Over the Top Southeast Asian blend

- Avocado oil, for drizzling

- Juice of half a lime

Instructions

  1. Plate sliced cucumber on serving platter or individual serving plates.
  2. Scatter scallion and serrano slices over the cucumber slices
  3. Portion the avocado slices decoratively (we like things to look nice)
  4. Squeeze the juice of half a lime, then drizzle the salad with avocado oil as desired
  5. Sprinkle a heaping tablespoon or so of Over the Top Southeast Asian, well, over the top
